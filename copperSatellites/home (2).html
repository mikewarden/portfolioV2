Bio:


Copper Satellites defies typical genre, combining elements of Industrial, Ambient, IDM, Synthwave into a rich sonic tapestry. Formed in the Summer of 2016, Copper Satellites is the electronic music brain-child of Alec Nelson and Mike Warden with the release of Ghost on Film following soon after. Their debut performance was at Island of Misfit Toys 31 in Los Angeles, CA July 4th, 2017. The second release Desert was released on a limited run for cassette as well as digital release.
