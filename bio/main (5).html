About Mike . . .




Mike was born and raised in Phoenix, AZ and has been involved in tech most of his life. Early in childhood, Mike was introduced to the internet in its dial-up infancy through DOS-based Prodigy and eventually the first Windows Browsers. Based on his background in operating systems and tech in general, Mike eventually got involved in electronic music production participating in a number of musical projects in a variety of genres over the years and contributed to the Phoenix art/music scene. He has also lived in Northern California for several years, having participated consistently in the music/art scene there as well. Mike is certified in Pro Tools HD, Logic Pro, System 6000 and AutoTune and a trained glassblower with over 5 years of full-time experience.
