About Mike Warden


Mike was born and raised in Phoenix, AZ and has been involved in tech most of his life. Early in childhood, Mike was introduced to the internet in its infancy, which consisted of DOS programs like Prodigy, or early browsers like with Netscape and built a few html mock-ups when he was around eleven years old. Based on his background in operating systems and tech in general, Mike eventually took a liking to electronic music production. He has applied knowledge of MIDI, signal flow, hardware VSTs and a strange musical aesthetic thusly, participating in a number of musical projects in a variety of genres over the years and contributed to the Phoenix art/music scene. Mike is also certified in Pro Tools HD, Logic Pro, System 6000 and AutoTune. Mike is a trained glassblower with over 5 years of full-time experience. He has also lived in Eureka, California for several years, having participated consistently in the music/art scene there as well.
